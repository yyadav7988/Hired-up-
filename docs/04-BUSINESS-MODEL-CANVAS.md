# Business Model Canvas — HiredUp

## 1. Key Partners
- Learning platforms (Coursera, Udemy, edX) for credential verification
- Recruitment agencies and staffing firms
- Universities and bootcamps for candidate pipeline

## 2. Key Activities
- Skill assessment development and maintenance
- Certificate verification engine
- AI credibility scoring
- Platform operations and support

## 3. Key Resources
- Assessment question bank
- ML scoring models
- Verification APIs and heuristics
- Engineering team

## 4. Value Propositions
- **For Recruiters:** Faster, data-driven shortlisting; reduced bias; verified credentials
- **For Candidates:** Transparent feedback; fair evaluation; skill-based opportunities
- **USP:** Unified skill-first hiring combining real-time testing, learning aptitude, and verified credentials

## 5. Customer Relationships
- Self-serve SaaS platform
- Onboarding and support
- Email/ticketing for enterprise

## 6. Channels
- Web app (primary)
- Direct sales (SMEs)
- Partnerships (recruitment agencies)

## 7. Customer Segments
- **Primary:** Startups and SMEs hiring technical talent
- **Secondary:** Recruitment agencies, HR departments

## 8. Cost Structure
- Infrastructure (hosting, DB)
- Development and R&D
- Certificate verification APIs (if paid)
- Support and sales

## 9. Revenue Streams
- **Freemium:** Free tier (limited assessments/jobs)
- **Subscription:** Per-recruiter or per-company monthly/annual
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing, white-label, integrations
