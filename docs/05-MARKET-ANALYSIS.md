# Market Analysis — HiredUp

## Market Size
- Global recruitment software market: ~$3.5B (2024), growing ~7% CAGR
- SME hiring tech underserved; many rely on spreadsheets and manual screening
- Technical talent shortage increases demand for efficient screening

## Problem
- Resume inflation and fake certifications
- Skill mismatch between candidates and roles
- Manual screening time: 6–10 hours per role
- Lack of affordable tools for startups

## Competitors
| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------------|-----------|------------|
| HackerRank | Strong coding tests | Limited to coding; no credential verification |
| Codility | Good assessment UX | No unified credibility score |
| LinkedIn | Large candidate pool | Self-reported skills; no verification |
| Greenhouse/Lever | Full ATS | Expensive; no AI scoring |

## Differentiation
- **HiredUp** combines: skill tests + certificate verification + learning aptitude + explainable AI score in one platform
- Target: Startups and SMEs (price-sensitive, need faster hiring)
- Transparent, explainable scoring (no black box)

## GTM Strategy
1. Free tier to acquire SMEs
2. Content marketing (hiring best practices)
3. Partnerships with bootcamps and learning platforms
